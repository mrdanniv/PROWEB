Professional Photographer

I shoot family portraits in the studio, work at fashion shows, photograph models for advertising, and cover weddings. A separate area is product and food photography, commissioned for ads or restaurant menus.

I also work as a crime scene photographer, a celebrity hunter, or capture wildlife for nature magazines. I'm a sought-after specialist in news publications — I work alongside journalists and can end up anywhere: from a glamorous party to a fire, flood, or armed conflict.
